Event Guidelines

To ensure a productive and enjoyable experience for all attendees, we kindly ask you to adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Professional Conduct: Maintain a respectful and professional demeanor throughout the event.
  • Networking Etiquette: Be mindful of others' time and engage in meaningful conversations.
  • Photography: Please refrain from taking photos or videos during presentations without prior consent.
  • Contact Information: Exchange contact information responsibly and respect others' privacy.
